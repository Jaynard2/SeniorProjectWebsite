Vanderbilt

Vanderbilt is a beautiful campus right in the middle of Nashville. The campus is also an arboretum, making the city campus feel as if it's right in the middle of nowhere. Most of the people seemed friendly, if a little uppity, and the tour was very nice. While I enjoyed the tour, the college is not for me. First of all, it is very expensive, at ~$70,000 a year. I also felt as if I wouldn't fit in - the college simply felt very "of the world", especially when compared to Cedarville.
