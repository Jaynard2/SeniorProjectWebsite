Conway's Game of Life Conway's Game of Life is a 2-demonsional grid made up of cells. Each cell is either "dead" or "alive", and there are several rules to determine wich it is. They are a) Any cell with fewer than 2 live neighbors die, b) Any cell with more than 3 live neigbors dies, and c) Any cell with exactly 3 live neigbors lives. The game starts by randomly assigning each cell a state (dead or alive). https://github.com/Jaynard2/LearningRaylib
